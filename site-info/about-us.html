About Us

Last Updated: September 27, 2021

Lockopedia is a labor of love. The website was started when Tom Montoya needed an idea for a project in his college web programming class. He decided to blend his love of picking locks, writing, and helping people to make a free resource for everybody.


The site is maintained by lock lovers, and anybody is welcome to contribute. All contributions are used to keep the sight running, to purchase and test new locks, and improve the quality of the website for everybody. This is a non-profit project, and nobody makes a penny from it.


Website Goals

  1. Present useful information, insights, and impartial information about locks, old and new, to all.
  2. Allow consumers, locksmiths, member of the lock sport community, and any other well-meaning people to post their insights and opinions about locks, lock picking, and lock smithing for all to read.
  3. Foster unity, good sportsmanship, professional development, transparency, and good will between professionals, developers, manufacturers, and consumers.
