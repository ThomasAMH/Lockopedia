Master Lock No. 3

One of Master Lock's most popular products. This small and affordable laminated padlock is used all over the United States in residential and low-security applications. It's main appeal is it's low price and wide availability, and it's critics cite it's poor core tolerances, weak body, several exploitable low-skill pick and bypass methods as main drawbacks to the lock's reliability.

Note: This product has several variations sold under the same name. See the Gallery at the bottom of the page to see more examples of the lock!
